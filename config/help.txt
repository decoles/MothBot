```detection:
    .detect <image link> - identifies object in image

Audio:
    .play <youtube link> - play sound
    .stop - stop playing current video 

Games:
    .8ball <question> - ask the magic 8ball a question
    .rps <rock/paper/scissors> - play rock paper scissors
    .roll <number> - roll a dice
    .flip - flip a coin
    
Other:
    .help - list all commands
    .ping - ping available servers
```
